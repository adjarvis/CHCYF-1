Chapel Hill Carrboro Youth Forward

The Chapel Hill-Carrboro Youth Services Initiative is an effort to help connect children and youth between the ages of five and 20 living in the Chapel Hill and Carrboro communities with nonprofit organizations serving youth. Born of a desire that all young people in Chapel Hill and Carrboro have access to programs and activities that would contribute to their growth and development into happy and productive citizens, the Chapel Hill-Carrboro Youth Services Initiative is supported by a group of local philanthropic organizations invested in the well-being of Chapel Hill and Carrboro children and youth.

This app is intended to serve as a resource for all community members - the youth and those working with youth - seeking opportunities and services that will help the youngest members of the Chapel Hill and Carrboro communities reach their greatest potential.

YMCA

The YMCA uses day camp, team leadership activities, and a variety of sports and aquatics to build character, self-confidence, and leadership in children. The YMCA seeks to put Christian principles into practice through programs that build healthy spirit, mind and body for all. The bulk of the Y’s service focuses on Health Living, providing recreational and fitness activities for all age groups. The Y also encourages Social Responsibility by providing adult and youth members with opportunities for giving and service. Lastly, the Y operates targeted Youth Development programming, including explicit youth leadership services.
